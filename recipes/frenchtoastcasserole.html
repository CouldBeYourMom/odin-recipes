French Toast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 loaves Brioche bread
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 tsp Vanilla
  • 10 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar

Method

  1. Combine all ingresients and mix will in very large bowl
  2. Add bread and coat completely with egg mixture
  3. Bake at 350 for about 40 minutes

Serve with powdered sugar, syrup, fruit, or bananas foster (recipe to follow.)
