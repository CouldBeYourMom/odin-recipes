Banana's Foster

Ingredients

  • 6 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 4 Bananas, sliced diagonally
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 Cup Dark Rum

Method

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet, add brown sugar then stir until incorporated
  2. Add bananas, salt, and vanilla then cook 3-5 minutes on one side until the bananas have developed a brown crust
  3. Flip slices over then cook for 1-2 more minutes
  4. Add rum and simmer for 1-2 minutes

Serve over ice cream, french toast, etc.
